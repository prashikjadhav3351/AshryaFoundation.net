Ashraya Foundation - What We Do

These are the steps to be followed at ASHRAYA Foundation for scholarships.

1

Apply for Scholarship:

- Gather necessary documents (transcripts, recommendation letters, etc.).

- Fill out the scholarship application form.

- Submit the application before the deadline.

2

Scholarship Eligibility Review:

- Review committee assesses applications.

- Applicants meeting criteria move to next stage.

- Applicants not meeting criteria are notified.

3

Aptitude Test:

- Eligible applicants are scheduled for an aptitude test.

- Test evaluates academic abilities, skills, and potential.

4

Interview:

- Successful test takers proceed to the interview round.

- Interview assesses candidates' motivation, goals, and suitability for the scholarship.

5

Fund Distribution Decision:

- Committee reviews test scores, interview performance, and overall application.

- Decide on scholarship recipients based on merit.

- Notify selected candidates.

6

Assign Mentors:

- Selected candidates are assigned with a mentor.

- Mentors are in constant touch with students and provide necessary guidance.

7

Student Career Tracking:

- Selected candidates enroll in the educational institution.

- Institution tracks academic progress and extracurricular activities.

- Regular check-ins and evaluations to monitor performance and ensure compliance with scholarship requirements.
